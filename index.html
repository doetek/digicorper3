Body Mass Index Calculator (BMI)

Body mass index calculator is used to estimate your total amount of fat. It is only an approximate measure of the best weight for your health.

What Your BMI Means

Once you have measured your BMI, you can determine your healthy weight range. If your BMI is:

  • Under 18.5 - you are very underweight and possibly malnourished.
  • Between 18.5 to 24.9 - you are a normal weight range for young and middle-aged adults.
  • Between 25.0 to 29.9 - you are overweight.
  • Over 30 - you are obese.
